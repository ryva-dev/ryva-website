# Mara Commercial Value Roadmap

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Status | Normative sequencing guide |
| Owner | Ryva Product |
| Last updated | 2026-07-16 |

## North star

Mara is an autonomous UGC operator. The creator films, approves, and handles genuinely human decisions. Mara absorbs the unpleasant work around earning money — research, targeting, contacts, pitches, creative direction, pipeline admin — without babysitting.

**Creator job:** film, approve, send when required, answer brand questions, fulfill deals.  
**Not the creator’s job:** find contacts, invent targets, babysit chat, update pipeline stages, or remember follow-ups.

## Hard sequencing rule

Do **not** begin Stage 1 infrastructure until Stage 0 evaluation demonstrates repeatably useful targeting, evidence-backed opportunities, verified contact paths (or honest contact blockers), and send-worthy pitches.

Ship failures honestly. Never ship fallback work as completed professional work.

---

## Stage 0 — Prove the work is worth receiving

**Goal:** Mara stops producing fake, generic, or commercially irrelevant work.

### Stage 0A — Stop dishonest / unusable output *(days, not a new epic)*

- Creator-stage-aware targeting (unknown stage fails closed as early)
- Dream brands are `build_toward`, never overnight primary targets
- No placeholder pitches (`[Your Name]`, `[Brand]`, …)
- No empty market pulses / empty digests presented as shipped
- No “I shipped X” when X is hidden from Deliverables
- No fabricated progress or calendar filler as Mara work
- Completed customer-facing work is visible in Deliverables
- Blockers are honest; Outlook/Gmail affordances do not break the layout

### Stage 0B — Prove good opportunities and pitches

Each delivered opportunity SHOULD include:

- Why this brand fits this creator
- Evidence the brand works with creators or needs relevant content
- Why it is reachable now
- Best contact route (or truthful contact blocker Mara still owns)
- Specific content angle
- Personalized pitch (no placeholders)
- Confidence and risks
- Exact next action

**Exit (numerical gate):** Across multiple overnight runs, **≥70%** of delivered opportunities are judged worth pursuing, and pitches require only minor edits. Adjust the threshold after first scored runs — do not remove the number.

**Do not start Stage 1 until this gate is green.**

---

## Stage 1 — Complete the pursuit loop

**Goal:** Turn worthwhile opportunities into sent outreach and tracked outcomes without chat.

Focus: autonomous contact discovery, confidence-tagged contacts, approve-ready pitches in Reviews, creator edit/approve, send or clear guided send, Gmail reply ingestion, reply classification, follow-ups, pipeline next action, Mara vs creator calendars.

Minimum product clarity (partial Stage 3) belongs here:

- Completed work visible
- Honest blockers
- Reviews easy to find
- Fake status gone
- Creator’s next action obvious

**Exit:** A paid stranger leaves Mara alone ~48h and returns to **≥2 opportunities they consider worth pursuing**, with verified contact paths and pitches they would send with little/no editing. Over the test period, **most approved opportunities are actually sent**.

Secondary metrics: approval rate, send rate, edit burden, replies, positive replies, paid conversations.

---

## Stage 2 — Improve the creator’s ability to win

Creative work only when it raises odds of getting or fulfilling a deal — never calendar filler.

**Exit:** Each meaningful opportunity can generate a filmable creative strategy creators understand without chatting.

---

## Stage 3 — Product feels like work was completed for them

Three surfaces: **Finished** · **Decisions** · **Momentum**. Chat is supplementary.

**Exit:** A new creator understands what Mara did, what matters, and what they must do next without opening Conversation.

---

## Stage 4 — Compounding judgment

Learn from approvals, edits, sends, replies, deals, logged payments, content performance, preferences, and behavior — with evidence-backed explanations.

**Exit:** Two different creators get materially different strategies; Mara can cite the evidence that caused the difference.

---

## Stage 5 — Prove retention and pricing *(before more workers / expansion)*

Validate: return without reminders, approve/send behavior, replies, perceived time saved, disappointment if removed, willingness to pay ~$79, time-to-value, cost-to-serve under deep work.

The product graduates when a small group uses it repeatedly and considers it worth paying for — not when the system merely functions.

---

## Codex / implementation instruction

**Implement Stage 0 only.** Do not begin Stage 1 infrastructure until the Stage 0 evaluation harness demonstrates repeatably useful targeting, evidence-backed opportunities, verified contact paths, and send-worthy pitches. Add measurable quality gates and expose failures honestly rather than shipping fallback work.
