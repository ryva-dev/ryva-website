# Mara Stage 0–1 Roadmap

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Status | Active implementation guide |
| Owner | Ryva Product |
| Last updated | 2026-07-16 |

## North star

Mara is an autonomous UGC operator. The creator films and approves. Mara owns research, targeting, contacts, pitches, creative intelligence, and pipeline admin — without babysitting.

## Stage 0 — Stop fake work

**Exit criteria**

- Early / unknown creator stage is treated as early (fail closed).
- Dream brands are `build_toward`, never primary overnight revenue work.
- Placeholder pitches (`[Your Name]`, `[Brand]`, …) never publish as shipped deliverables.
- Empty market pulses (no opportunities / no signals) do not publish as shipped work.
- Digests and “While you were away” use the same publication rules as office deliverables.

## Stage 1 — Close the money loop

**Exit criteria**

- Contact discovery stays Mara-owned; creator Confirm CTA only when a confirmable inferred contact exists.
- Personalized, quality-gated pitches for outreach-ready brands land in Reviews / approvals when sendable.
- Autonomy prioritizes commercial progress (research → contact → pitch) over cooldown filler.
- Pipeline next actions do not ask the creator to hunt contacts.
- Calendar defaults: Mara owns research/prep; creator owns film/approve/send.

## Non-goals (later stages)

Video critique depth, full social platform analysis UX redesign, Phase 5 office rewrite, legacy planner deletion.
