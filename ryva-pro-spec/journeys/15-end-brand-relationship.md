# Journey 15: End a Brand Relationship

**Trigger:** Agreement expiry/termination, Brand/Representative decision, material breach, persistent unworkability, or portfolio decision.

**Required records:** Brand, active Agreement/Territory, open Opportunities, Accounts/Protection, Orders/Reorders, Commissions/Disputes, Contacts, documents, end authority/reason.

## Flow

1. User starts relationship-end review and selects effective date/reason.
2. System lists dependencies and contractual/post-termination conditions.
3. AI may summarize records and missing closure actions; no legal conclusion.
4. User resolves/cancels external drafts/sequences, assigns communication and handoff.
5. User personally approves required Brand/Buyer communications.
6. System ends Agreement/territory on effective date, stops unauthorized future outreach, changes Brand to Ended.
7. Open Opportunities close/reassign according to evidence.
8. Protection, reorder rights, earned/unpaid Commissions and disputes follow their documented terms; history remains.
9. User completes data/export/return-of-material obligations.

**Automation:** dependency checklist, scheduled access/authority stop, reminders, sequence cancellation.

**Approvals:** Human end decision and communications; specialist review if contract ambiguous.

**Success:** relationship ends without losing obligations, evidence, account history, or earned compensation.

**Failure:** unresolved active order/account, unclear protection/post-term commission, queued send, missing authority.

**Recovery:** pause pending resolution, narrow effective scope, specialist review, reconcile queued jobs, reopen only with new Agreement.

**Audit events:** decision/reason, dependency actions, communications, agreement/status changes, protection, commissions, archive/export.
