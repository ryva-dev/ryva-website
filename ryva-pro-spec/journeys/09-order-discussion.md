# Journey 9: Move Into Order Discussion

**Trigger:** Buyer Review produces credible interest in terms, quantity, Products, timing, or opening Order.

**Required records:** Opportunity, authorized Buyer, active Agreement, Products/readiness, proposed terms, current price/materials, open conditions, estimates, Activities.

## Flow

1. User records Buyer interest and decision participants.
2. System validates authority to discuss versus bind, current pricing/readiness, territory, conflict, and claims.
3. User captures Products, quantities/ranges, timing, delivery, returns, payment, support, and unresolved questions.
4. AI may compare terms, summarize open conditions, calculate labeled estimates, and prepare questions.
5. User conducts negotiation personally within authority.
6. System moves to Terms or Order Discussion after human confirmation.
7. Parties resolve conditions; user records proposed/accepted terms without calling them final prematurely.

**Automation:** term checklist, estimate/commission recalculation, deadline task, stale/open-condition alert.

**Approvals:** Human negotiation, concessions, and final interpretation; specialist review if contract/category issue.

**Success:** authorized parties reach documented order terms ready for verified Order, or decline professionally.

**Failure:** authority exceeded, terms ambiguous/unworkable, readiness/fulfillment change, Buyer not authorized, conflict.

**Recovery:** clarify/escalate, return to Buyer Review/Qualify, revise Product mix, or close lost/disqualify.

**Audit events:** interest, stage, term versions, estimates, concessions, approvals, decisions.
