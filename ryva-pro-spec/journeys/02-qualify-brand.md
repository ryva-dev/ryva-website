# Journey 2: Research and Qualify a Brand

**Trigger:** Product investigation requires Brand diligence or user adds a Brand.

**Required records:** Brand, Products, Sources/Evidence, Contacts if known, Risk Flags, Representation Opportunity, Decision Record.

## Flow

1. User opens Brand in Discovered/Researching.
2. System shows identity, ownership, wholesale, distribution, operations, communication, claims, Product, and support gaps.
3. User gathers authorized evidence and verifies identity/contact sources.
4. AI summarizes evidence, extracts claims/operations, suggests missing fields and contradictions.
5. User evaluates legitimacy, readiness, operations, communication, risks, and Product separation.
6. Blocking risk opens specialist or evidence task.
7. User records scoped human decision: Contact Ready, continue research, or reject.
8. System validates Contact purpose and risk before Contact Ready.

**Automation:** evidence freshness, duplicate Brand, Contact candidates, risk flags, next task.

**Approvals:** Human Brand qualification; specialist where claims/safety/legal/financial issue genuinely requires it.

**Success:** Brand reaches Contact Ready with verified-enough identity, reason, Contact route, evidence confidence, risks and next action.

**Failure:** fraud/authority failure, unresolved material risk, inability to support representation, insufficient evidence.

**Recovery:** remain Researching, request correction, specialist review, or reject with new-case reopening rule.

**Audit events:** evidence, Contact verification, risks, decision, stage, specialist task, AI disposition.
