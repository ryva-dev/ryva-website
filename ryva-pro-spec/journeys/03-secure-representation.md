# Journey 3: Contact a Brand and Secure Representation

**Trigger:** Brand is Contact Ready and the Representative wants to propose representation.

**Required records:** Brand, Contact, Representation Opportunity, Products, Evidence, outreach draft/approval, Activities, proposed Agreement/Territory.

## Flow

1. User prepares Brand-specific approach from qualified evidence.
2. AI may draft and personalize with source-backed limits.
3. System validates identity, Contact permission, claims, active credential and no stop risk.
4. User reviews exact communication and approves send.
5. System sends/logs, creates follow-up, tracks reply/opt-out.
6. User conducts discovery and records Brand objectives, scope, Products, territory, support, conflicts, economics, and authority.
7. Brand provides proposed agreement/terms.
8. AI extracts candidate fields and missing/ambiguous terms.
9. User negotiates personally and uploads final executed/approved document.
10. User confirms extracted fields, authority, commission, protection/reorder rights, term, and conflicts.
11. System creates active Agreement/Territory and advances Brand to Authorized/Active.

**Automation:** follow-up, reply stop, term-gap alerts, expiry reminders, conflict scan.

**Approvals:** exact outreach; human agreement acceptance; specialist contract review when warranted.

**Success:** active verified Agreement with explicit scope, or relationship professionally declined.

**Failure:** no reply, opt-out, unacceptable/unclear terms, no authority, conflict, specialist stop.

**Recovery:** approved follow-up cadence; request clarification; revise terms; pause; close/reject. Never outreach beyond authority.

**Audit events:** every communication version/result, discovery, term extraction/edit, approvals, agreement activation, stage.
