# Journey 4: Find a Suitable Business Buyer

**Trigger:** Active representation exists and a Product is ready for target research.

**Required records:** Agreement/Territory, Product/Brand, Business candidates, Sources/Evidence, Contacts/Buyer roles, fit rationale, Risk/Conflict.

## Flow

1. User defines target Business type, geography, channel, price/assortment/operational criteria.
2. System searches existing workspace and optional approved providers.
3. AI suggests candidate Businesses and Product matches with evidence and unknowns.
4. User reviews identity, assortment, customers, price, operations, geography, vendors, fit and irrelevant/personal-data risks.
5. System checks Agreement territory, existing Opportunities, Accounts, and Protected Account conflicts.
6. User qualifies or rejects each Business and verifies/creates Buyer Contact.
7. System records fit rationale and next research/action.

**Automation:** duplicate Business/Contact, conflict check, Contact freshness, missing-fit evidence.

**Approvals:** Human Business qualification and match thesis. No external outreach yet.

**Success:** qualified Business and decision path with supported fit rationale, Contact state, no unresolved conflict, and next action.

**Failure:** poor fit, no legitimate purpose, authority/territory failure, opt-out, conflict, unverifiable Business/Contact.

**Recovery:** refine target criteria, gather evidence, choose different Business/Product, resolve conflict; do not mass-pitch.

**Audit events:** search/import source, AI suggestions, qualification, fit rationale, Contact verification, conflict, rejection.
