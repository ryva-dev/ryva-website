# Analytics and Reporting

## Principles

- actuals, estimates, and forecasts are visually and structurally separate;
- definitions are accessible from every metric;
- filters show time zone, currency, date basis, and included records;
- activities do not substitute for commercial or relationship outcomes;
- numerical Product/Brand/Fit/Opportunity scores are excluded;
- charts have accessible table equivalents;
- corrections and late-arriving data recompute with history.

## Representative performance dashboard

Metrics:

- active Brands and Product lines;
- active Placement Opportunities by stage;
- approved outreach volume;
- response and meaningful-conversation rates;
- information/sample-sent rate;
- opening orders;
- reorder rate;
- verified wholesale order value;
- expected, approved, payable, paid, disputed, canceled, and clawed-back commissions;
- median/average sales cycle with definition;
- stalled Opportunities;
- overdue next actions;
- evidence and authority blockers.

Filters: period, Brand, Product, Business type, stage, geography, source, account, actual/estimate.

## Product performance

- represented Products;
- Opportunities and Buyer interest;
- response/conversation rates;
- opening orders;
- reorder incidence;
- verified order value;
- commission contribution;
- effective Buyer categories;
- return/damage/complaint patterns;
- evidence freshness and risk flags.

Do not rank Product quality from these outcomes alone.

## Brand performance

- representation and active period;
- Opportunities and accounts opened;
- active/at-risk accounts;
- order and reorder activity;
- commission approval/payment reliability;
- overdue payments and disputes;
- fulfillment outcomes;
- communication response evidence;
- Representative support;
- relationship health.

## Portfolio health

- Brand, Account, Product, and paid-commission concentration;
- recurring versus one-time verified income;
- overdue commissions;
- inactive/at-risk accounts;
- upcoming reorder reviews;
- conflict and protection-expiry risk;
- relationship depth;
- Representative capacity and overdue commitments.

No universal ideal portfolio count is shown.

## Forecasting

Initial production forecasting includes:

- low/base/high expected order value entered by user;
- expected commission derived from current agreement and base estimate;
- projected reorder window and value range;
- qualitative likelihood: Early, Possible, Supported, Strong, with evidence;
- account renewal/protection risk.

Weighted pipeline is disabled by default because stage probabilities are unvalidated.

Every forecast displays:

- estimate label;
- horizon;
- input values and source;
- assumptions;
- confidence;
- owner;
- last updated;
- comparison with actual when outcome arrives.

## Commission reporting

Reconciliation table:

- Order gross value;
- discounts;
- returns/cancellations;
- net commissionable amount;
- rule/rate;
- expected;
- approved;
- payable;
- paid;
- disputed/clawed back;
- variance and explanation.

## Exports

CSV and PDF/print-ready summaries where permitted. Export records metric definitions, filters, time, currency, and actual/estimate status.

## Data freshness

Dashboard header shows last calculated time and provider health. Partial data is labeled. Provider failure never displays zero as if no activity occurred.
