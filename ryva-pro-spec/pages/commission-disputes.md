# Page: Commission Disputes

## Purpose and user

Manage evidence and resolution of disputed commission basis, eligibility, amount, due date, payment, or clawback.

## Data displayed

Dispute ID/status, Commission, Brand/Account/Order, disputed amount/reason, agreement/protection terms, evidence, timeline, next action/owner, due dates, Brand response, proposed/final resolution.

## Actions

Primary: Open/Submit/Resolve or Withdraw according to authority/state.  
Secondary: add evidence, request clarification, log communication, adjust claimed amount, create task, export case file.

## Filters

Status, reason, Brand, due/age, amount, evidence-needed, resolution.

## States

- **Empty:** no disputes; link overdue/variance Commissions.
- **Loading:** status and deadlines first.
- **Error:** preserve draft/evidence; do not alter Commission status incorrectly.

## Permissions and responsive

Representative; Ryva does not adjudicate contractual rights unless an approved policy creates that service. Admin/support access controlled. Mobile supports evidence and communication log.

## Linked records and AI

Commission, Order, Agreement, Protection, Documents, Activities, Tasks. AI summarizes differences and extracts terms; human owns claim/resolution.

## Acceptance criteria

- opening sets Commission Disputed;
- complete evidence and chronology retained;
- resolved amount and rationale required;
- withdrawal does not imply Brand correctness;
- no AI resolution;
- export is complete and auditable.
