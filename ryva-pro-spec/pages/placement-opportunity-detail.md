# Page: Placement Opportunity Detail

## Purpose and user

Provide the operational record for one Brand–Product–Business pursuit from identification through account and reorder.

## Data displayed

Header: stage, stalled/conflict/risk, owner, last activity, next action, estimate, confidence.  
Core panel: Brand, Products, Business, Buyers/Contacts, Agreement/territory, match thesis, Relationship Triangle, decision and evidence.  
Tabs:

- Overview;
- Preparation and outreach;
- Buyer needs/information/samples;
- terms and opening Order;
- Account/protection;
- reorders and commissions;
- activity/timeline;
- tasks, notes, documents;
- evidence, decisions, approvals, audit.

## Actions

Primary: current next action.  
Secondary: validate/transition stage, add evidence/task/note, draft/approve outreach, log reply/call/sample, update estimate, record Order, close/disqualify/reopen.

## Filters

Timeline/activity; evidence class; task; Product; Contact; document; decision version.

## States

- **Empty:** section-specific missing requirement and action.
- **Loading:** stage, authority, conflict and next action first.
- **Error:** consequential actions blocked if authority/conflict cannot load.

## Permissions and responsive

Representative. Mobile fully supports next action, timeline, Contacts, tasks, notes, stage, approvals, call/send, Order/commission summary.

## Linked records and AI

All connected records. AI prepares briefs, drafts, objections, summaries, evidence gaps, and next actions with sources.

## Acceptance criteria

- record tells why Opportunity exists and what happens next;
- outreach requires exact approval;
- Terms/Order discussion cannot imply agreement;
- Opening Order requires verified source;
- account/protection/commission creation is explainable;
- history cannot be silently edited.
