# Page: Business Detail

## Purpose and user

Provide the complete commercial context for one Business, its locations, Buyers, assortment, fit, Opportunities, accounts, and relationship history.

## Data displayed

Header: identity, type/category, locations, owner, qualification, account/protection/conflict, next action, risks.  
Tabs:

- Overview and sourced summary;
- Locations/geography;
- Assortment, customer, price positioning, vendors;
- Contacts and Buyer roles;
- Product-match hypotheses;
- Placement Opportunities;
- Account, orders, reorders, commissions;
- Outreach/activity;
- Evidence, tasks, notes, documents, history.

## Actions

Primary: Qualify Business / Perform next action.  
Secondary: add/verify Contact, add location/evidence, evaluate Product match, create Opportunity if authorized, log activity, protect account through workflow, merge/archive.

## Filters

Location; Contact status/role; Product/Brand; Opportunity stage; activity; order period; risk.

## States

- **Empty:** tab-specific prompts; no Contact is not represented as no Buyer.
- **Loading:** identity/account conflict first.
- **Error:** conflict and opt-out state fail closed if unavailable.

## Permissions and responsive

Representative. Sensitive Contacts and commercial records follow workspace rights. Mobile prioritizes Contacts, current Opportunities, next action, notes, account status.

## Linked records and AI

All Business relations. AI suggests match rationale, Contact roles, summary, and gaps; no final Buyer qualification or outreach.

## Acceptance criteria

- Business identity and Buyer authority remain separate;
- personal social data is excluded unless professionally relevant and lawful;
- Product matches show evidence and unknowns;
- account/protection status derives from approved records;
- current vendors marked source/known date;
- changes audited.
