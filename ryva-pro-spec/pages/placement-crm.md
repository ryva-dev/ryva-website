# Page: Placement CRM

## Purpose and user

Operate the full Placement Opportunity pipeline with next-action clarity, evidence gates, risk, account conflict, and commercial context.

## Data displayed

Views: Kanban, table, timeline, calendar, saved filtered views.  
Every card/row shows Product(s), Brand, Business Buyer, primary Contact, stage, last activity, next action, days in stage, expected order range, expected commission estimate, account conflict, risks, evidence confidence, owner, stalled flag.

## Actions

Primary: Create Opportunity / Perform selected next action.  
Secondary: stage transition, assign, add task/note/activity, prepare outreach, log sample, update estimate, close/disqualify/reopen, export.

Drag-and-drop validates rules and opens required-field/reason panel. No transition commits on drop alone when consequential.

## Filters

Stage, owner, Brand, Product, Business type, geography, Contact, stalled, risk, conflict, confidence, last/next activity, estimate range, account status. Saved views.

Bulk actions: assign, task, tag, stale review, archive closed; no bulk stage advancement, outreach, close, disqualify, order, or approval.

## States

- **Empty:** explain Opportunity definition and route to authority/Product/Business setup.
- **Loading:** stage columns and counts stable.
- **Error:** failed transition preserves original stage and input; partial provider data labeled.

## Permissions and responsive

Representative. Read-only states disable mutations. Mobile renders stage-grouped list and validated stage sheet.

## Linked records and AI

All Opportunity relations. AI detects stalls, gaps, match candidates and next actions; no silent stage change or close.

## Acceptance criteria

- cannot create valid Opportunity without agreement, Brand, Product, Business, match thesis, owner, next action;
- stage requirements enforced;
- account conflicts block outreach;
- estimates labeled and actuals separate;
- loss/disqualification/reopen rules apply;
- every transition audited.
