# Page: Home

## Purpose and user

Provide the Representative command center: what matters now, what changed, what is blocked, and what action creates the strongest responsible value.

## Data displayed

### Metrics

- due and overdue tasks;
- stalled Opportunities;
- active Opportunities by stage;
- accounts in reorder window;
- expected/approved/payable/overdue commissions;
- critical evidence, authority, conflict, and risk blockers.

### Cards

- Today's Priorities: maximum seven ranked items;
- Changes Since Last Visit;
- Overdue Follow-ups;
- Stalled Opportunities;
- Evidence and Risk Review;
- Reorder Watch;
- Commission Summary;
- Recent Activity;
- Onboarding/Setup State when incomplete.

Each item shows reason, source, owner, due time, linked record, and next action.

## Actions

Primary: perform or open next action.  
Secondary: complete/snooze task with reason, approve/reject suggestion, open record, adjust Home preferences.

## Filters

Today/7 days; Brand; Product; Business type; Opportunity stage; owner; risk; commission state. Saved Home preferences persist.

## Priority logic

1. credential/security/conflict blocks;
2. commitments due/overdue;
3. Buyer or Brand time-sensitive responses;
4. opening-order/account handoff;
5. reorder and commission deadlines;
6. stalled Opportunities;
7. evidence/qualification;
8. setup and long-horizon research.

AI may suggest within policy but cannot elevate commission over fit or trust.

## States

- **Empty:** distinguish new workspace (“Add first Product/Brand/Business”) from no urgent work (“Review portfolio or research queue”).
- **Loading:** stable card skeletons; partial provider data labeled.
- **Error:** affected card shows failure/freshness; other cards remain usable.

## Permissions and responsive

Representative sees own workspace. Read-only access shows status and disables actions. Mobile shows prioritized list, not full metric grid.

## Linked records and AI

Links all workspaces. AI supplies explainable weekly/daily priority suggestions, change summaries, and missing-data prompts with evidence.

## Acceptance criteria

- every priority has reason and next action;
- expected commissions are labeled estimates;
- no card treats no provider data as zero;
- critical blockers cannot be silently dismissed;
- Home loads useful internal data even if AI/providers fail;
- user actions and suggestion dispositions are audited.
