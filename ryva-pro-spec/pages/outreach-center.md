# Page: Outreach Center

## Purpose and user

Prepare, approve, execute, and review professional communication while keeping authority, Contact permission, Buyer context, and full history visible.

## Data displayed

Unified activity/inbox view: draft/approved/sent/received email, calls, voicemails, social-message drafts/logs, tasks, notes, sequence steps, replies, opt-outs, failures. Context rail shows Contact, Business, Brand, Products, Opportunity, stage, match thesis, approved claims, risk, last/next action.

## Actions

Primary: Compose / Review Next Communication.  
Secondary: log call, prepare call, create task/note, enroll in sequence, stop sequence, classify reply, set opt-out, schedule reminder, link/unlink ambiguous activity.

## Composer

Recipient, from identity, channel, subject/body, attachments, template, source-backed personalization, claim/evidence review, scheduling, explicit approval then send.

## Call workspace

Buyer/Brand brief, objectives, questions, objections, limits, call/voicemail script, call initiation, outcome, notes, next action.

## Filters

Channel, direction, state, Contact, Brand, Business, Opportunity, sequence, date, reply/opt-out, approval, owner. Saved views.

## States

- **Empty:** connect email or log manual activity; explain approval.
- **Loading:** cached history labeled while sync completes.
- **Error:** provider health/reconnect; manual logging; uncertain sends reconciled before retry.

## Permissions and responsive

Representative only sends own approved communication. Mobile fully supports review/send, call, notes, reply and tasks.

## Linked records and AI

Contacts, Businesses/Brands, Opportunities, sequences/templates, tasks, evidence, approvals. AI drafts, personalizes, summarizes and suggests objections/follow-up; cannot send.

## Acceptance criteria

- no outreach without active credential, authority, permission, no blocking conflict, and exact human approval;
- sent content hash matches approved version;
- reply/opt-out stops eligible sequences;
- provider failures do not duplicate sends;
- AI claims link evidence;
- complete communication history is auditable.
