# Page: Business and Buyer Intelligence

## Purpose and user

Find, qualify, organize, and match legitimate commercial Businesses and decision-makers without mass-pitching.

## Data displayed

Business name, type, category, locations, geography, assortment, target customer, price positioning, relevant Products/vendors, Contact/Buyer verification, fit hypotheses, active Opportunities, account/protection/conflict state, last activity, next action, risks.

Supported Business types include independent retailers, boutiques, salons, spas, gyms, hotels, corporate gifting, subscription boxes, distributors, specialty ecommerce, regional retailers, and other qualified Business buyers.

## Actions

Primary: Add/Save Business or Start Qualification.  
Secondary: discover/verify Contact, add evidence, suggest Product match, create list/task, map, create Opportunity if gates pass, merge/archive.

## Views and filters

Table, card/list, map, saved lists. Filters: type/category, geography/radius, locations, assortment, price positioning, Buyer role/verification, fit state, Product/Brand, Opportunity stage, account/protection/conflict, activity/freshness, risk.

Bulk prospecting permits adding to research list, assigning tasks, or preparing individual drafts after gates. No bulk external send without per-recipient review and approval.

## States

- **Empty:** import/add/discover with qualification explanation.
- **Loading:** partial enrichment labeled.
- **Error:** external discovery failure does not remove user records; retry and manual path.

## Permissions and responsive

Representative. Mobile supports search, map/list, Buyer lookup, notes, task, and quick qualification. Advanced list/filter desktop-first.

## Linked records and AI

Businesses, Contacts, Buyers, Products, Brands, Opportunities, Accounts, Protected Accounts, Activities, Evidence. AI proposes matches/Contacts and summarizes; Representative verifies and owns fit.

## Acceptance criteria

- no Business is qualified from protected/personal traits;
- Contact source and verification visible;
- fit is Product/Brand/context-specific;
- protected-account conflict checked before Opportunity/outreach;
- bulk workflow preserves individual human review;
- map has list alternative.
