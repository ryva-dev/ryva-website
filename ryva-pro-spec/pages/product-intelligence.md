# Page: Product Intelligence

## Purpose and user

Discover, evaluate, save, monitor, qualify, reject, and represent Products. Primary user is the Certified Representative.

## Data displayed

Views: Discover, Watchlist, Under Review, Qualified, Rejected, Represented, Recently Updated.  
Columns/cards: image, Product, Brand, category, qualification, readiness, evidence confidence/freshness, review volume/quality, price, trend direction, physical retail presence, critical risk, last update, owner, next action.

TikTok Shop indicators appear only when legally/technically available and show platform, period, geography, acquisition context, refunds/repeats where available, source, and limitations.

## Actions

Primary: Save Product / Start Review.  
Secondary: compare, add evidence, qualify/reject through human decision, watch/unwatch, link Brand/Opportunity, archive, export permitted view.

## Views and filters

Table, card, comparison launch, saved views. Filters: status, category, Brand, source, evidence confidence/freshness, readiness, risk, price band, retail presence, trend direction, updated date.

Bulk actions: owner/tag/watchlist/task/archive; no bulk qualification/rejection or evidence classification.

## States

- **Empty:** view-specific action—Discover source, import, or add manually.
- **Loading:** stable rows/cards; source health visible.
- **Error:** partial source failure labeled; saved records remain available.

## Permissions and responsive

Representative workspace only. Read-only users cannot mutate. Mobile supports search/watch/quick review; table configuration and comparison are desktop-first.

## Linked records and AI

Brand, Evidence, Documents, risks, readiness, Representation and Placement Opportunities. AI summarizes, detects gaps, proposes Buyer categories and comparisons; no final qualification or numerical score.

## Acceptance criteria

- Unknown never appears as neutral;
- Product and Brand qualification remain separate;
- source and freshness reachable from every material indicator;
- viral/social metrics cannot dominate layout or imply demand;
- human decision required for Qualified/Rejected;
- recently updated explains what changed.
