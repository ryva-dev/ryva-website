# Product Vision

## Purpose

Ryva Pro helps certified Brand Placement Representatives make analytical, evidence-supported decisions about Products, Brands, and Business Buyers so they spend less time researching and organizing and more time building commercial relationships.

It combines the analytical utility expected from a professional market terminal with the connected workflow discipline of a purpose-built CRM. It is not a generic CRM with renamed fields.

## Core user outcome

On opening Ryva Pro, the Representative can determine:

- which Products deserve attention;
- which Brands are ready for diligence or contact;
- which Businesses are the best-supported fit;
- which approved outreach happens next;
- which Opportunities are progressing, blocked, or stalled;
- which accounts require support or reorder review;
- which commissions are estimated, pending verification, approved, payable, paid, disputed, canceled, or clawed back; and
- which actions create the strongest long-term three-party value.

## Product promise

Ryva Pro maintains one connected professional record across:

`evidence → Product → Brand → representation authority → Business → Buyer → Opportunity → outreach → order → account → reorder → commission`

Every consequential state is explainable, owned, dated, and auditable.

## Inspiration translated into Ryva

| Proven pattern | Ryva adaptation |
|---|---|
| Simple visual pipeline | Placement Cycle-aware CRM with strict evidence gates and next action |
| Connected CRM records | Typed Product, Brand, Business, mandate, Opportunity, account, order, and commission relationships |
| Built-in outreach | Human-approved email, call preparation, sequences, and complete communication history |
| Business/contact intelligence | Evidence-linked Business and decision-maker discovery with verification state |
| Flexible relationships | Typed relational objects plus bounded custom fields and saved views |
| Analytics and forecasting | Actual-versus-estimate separation, pipeline health, portfolio concentration, and commission transparency |
| Commission operations | Explainable calculation lineage, approval, payment, disputes, and clawbacks |

## North-star product behavior

The interface directs attention to the next responsible action, not the most activity. It makes missing evidence, conflict, authority, risk, and overdue commitments difficult to ignore.

## Success measures

Primary:

- qualified Opportunities reaching meaningful Buyer conversation;
- opening orders with complete evidence and authority;
- healthy reorders and active accounts;
- verified commission captured and paid;
- time from signal to responsible next action;
- reduction in overdue and unowned commitments.

Guardrail:

- poor-fit outreach rate;
- stage reversals caused by missing authority;
- unsupported claim incidents;
- account conflicts;
- disputed commissions;
- stale critical evidence;
- AI suggestions materially corrected or rejected;
- data and access incidents.

Activity volume is diagnostic, not success.
