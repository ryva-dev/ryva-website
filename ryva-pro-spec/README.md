# Ryva Pro Product Specification

**Version:** Complete first-production draft 0.1  
**Status:** Ready for Founder review and implementation planning  
**Product:** Ryva Pro  
**Access:** Active eligible Ryva Brand Placement Certification required  
**Architecture:** TypeScript modular monolith

Ryva Pro is the operating system for Brand Placement Representatives. This specification defines the connected workspaces, records, workflows, permissions, automations, AI behavior, analytics, responsive behavior, pages, journeys, acceptance tests, and build sequence for the first production version.

## Authority

In order of precedence:

1. applicable law and binding obligations;
2. [The Ryva Standard](../the-ryva-standard/README.md);
3. [The Ryva Frameworks](../ryva-frameworks/README.md);
4. this product specification;
5. implementation plans and code.

The [Framework Pilot](../framework-pilot/README.md) informs the data and human-review model. It does not authorize production numerical scores.

## Product workspaces

- Home
- Product Intelligence
- Brand Intelligence
- Business and Buyer Intelligence
- Placement CRM
- Outreach Center
- Accounts, Orders, Reorders, and Commissions
- Analytics
- Tasks, Calendar, Documents, Notifications, Search, and Settings

## Explicit exclusions

- production numerical intelligence scores;
- autonomous external communication;
- automatic contract approval or negotiation;
- Brand-side portal;
- social feed or community surface;
- Opportunity marketplace allocation;
- native mobile app;
- custom no-code object builder;
- payroll, invoicing, or accounting system;
- legal, regulatory, tax, safety, or financial conclusions by AI;
- public rankings or badges based on internal intelligence.

## Definition of complete

The first production version is complete when a certified Representative can move from Product discovery through Brand authorization, Buyer qualification, approved outreach, opening order, protected account, reorder, and explainable commission tracking with connected evidence, human decisions, audit history, export, and safe credential lifecycle.
