# RPD-008: Retention Periods

**Default:** Implement entity retention categories and deletion/hold mechanisms; set exact durations only after qualified privacy, legal, financial, contractual, provider, and market review.

**Alternative:** One universal retention period.

**Reason:** Agreements, communications, Contacts, financial records, evidence, and security logs have different purposes and obligations.

**Revisit when:** Initial launch market, provider set, contracts, and legal entity are fixed.
