# RPD-003: Certified Closer

**Default:** Do not create Certified Closer as a product role or credential. If a Closing Program is approved, store completion as a capability/training flag on a Certified Representative.

**Alternative:** Separate credential and access role.

**Reason:** The Standard establishes Representative and Advanced Sales Program, not Certified Closer.

**Revisit when:** The Standard formally defines the title, competency, certification claim, renewal, and product rights.
