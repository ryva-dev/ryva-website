# RPD-007: Support Access

**Default:** No impersonation. Support access is ticket-scoped, time-boxed, field-restricted, reasoned, and audited; sensitive content remains redacted unless necessary.

**Alternative:** General read access or impersonation for fast troubleshooting.

**Reason:** Protects confidential commercial relationships and user trust.

**Revisit when:** Operational evidence shows a specific support need that cannot be met with diagnostics and scoped grants.
