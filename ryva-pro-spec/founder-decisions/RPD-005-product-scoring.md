# RPD-005: Production Numerical Scores

**Default:** Exclude numerical Product, Brand, Business Fit, Opportunity, Portfolio, and Relationship scores. Use categorical qualification, evidence confidence, risk, human decision, and next action.

**Alternative:** Provisional visible unweighted scores.

**Reason:** Pilot findings state production scoring is not ready.

**Revisit when:** Framework weights, gates, reviewer agreement, validation, bias, missing-data and user-comprehension tests are approved.
