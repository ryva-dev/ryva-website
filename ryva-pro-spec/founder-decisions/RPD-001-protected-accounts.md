# RPD-001: Protected Account Authority

**Default:** Implement Protected Account as a record of rights stated in a specific Representation Agreement or approved supporting document. Require scope, term, territory/channel/Product coverage, commission/reorder rights, evidence, conflict check, and human approval.

**Alternative:** Treat account protection as a Ryva-created platform right independent of Brand agreements.

**Reason:** The default is truthful, reversible, and avoids Ryva manufacturing legal/commercial rights.

**Revisit when:** Founder approves a network-wide account-protection policy, standardized Brand agreements, adjudication, and appeals.
