# RPD-002: Credential Expiry Grace

**Default:** 30-day read-only grace after expiry. Renewal, export, subscription, certification and support remain available; operational changes, AI generation and outreach are blocked.

**Alternative:** Immediate block or continued full access during renewal.

**Reason:** Protects certification gating while allowing continuity and data retrieval.

**Revisit when:** Certification renewal rules, user behavior, contractual commitments, and support evidence justify another period.
