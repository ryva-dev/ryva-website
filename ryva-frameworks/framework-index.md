# Framework Index

## Selection by decision

| If the question is… | Use | Do not substitute |
|---|---|---|
| Where is this work in the lifecycle? | RF-01 Placement Cycle | A generic sales pipeline |
| Does the relationship create value for all parties? | RF-02 Relationship Triangle | Commission or opening-order size |
| Should I advocate for this Brand or Product? | RF-03 Standard of Recommendation | Product Score alone |
| Is this Product worth investigating? | RF-04 Product Score | Brand or Business Fit Score |
| Is this Brand ready and suitable to represent? | RF-05 Brand Score | Product popularity |
| Does this offer fit this specific Business? | RF-06 Business Fit Score | Prospect volume |
| Can the offer be presented operationally? | RF-07 Retail Readiness Standard | Online popularity |
| How should this specific Opportunity be prioritized? | RF-08 Opportunity Score | Estimated revenue alone |
| Is the overall book of work healthy? | RF-09 Portfolio Framework | Number of Brands or accounts |
| What value exists beyond the opening order? | RF-10 Relationship Value Model | Projected revenue labeled as actual |
| Should this company, product, or practice decision proceed? | RF-11 Decision Filter | Informal preference |
| What supports this claim? | RF-12 Evidence Standard | Source popularity |
| What must a person decide? | RF-13 Human Judgment Standard | Technical ability to automate |

## Dependencies

```text
Evidence Standard + Human Judgment Standard
                  ↓
Product Score + Brand Score + Retail Readiness
                  ↓
Business Fit Score + Relationship Triangle
                  ↓
Standard of Recommendation
                  ↓
Opportunity Score
                  ↓
Placement Cycle
                  ↓
Portfolio Framework + Relationship Value Model

Decision Filter governs adoption and exceptions across the set.
```

## Framework status

All frameworks are first drafts. Their structures are suitable for controlled prototyping. Numerical weights, thresholds, outcome bands, mandatory gates, and public claims remain subject to the [Founder Decision Log](05-governance/founder-decision-log.md), validation, and formal approval.

## Shared output fields

Every material framework result should preserve:

- framework ID and version;
- evaluated object and context;
- result date and responsible person;
- evidence references and observation dates;
- fact, proxy, estimate, assumption, inference, and unknown classifications;
- criterion results and missing evidence;
- confidence label and rationale;
- warnings, gates, and overrides;
- recommended next action and owner; and
- review or expiry condition.
