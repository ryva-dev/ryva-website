## Decision record requirements

Each decision must identify:

- accountable owner and authority;
- affected Standard and Framework provisions;
- facts, evidence, assumptions, and specialist review;
- alternatives and three-party effects;
- implementation, migration, and communication;
- version and effective date; and
- review or expiry trigger.
