# Terminology Dictionary and Glossary

Capitalized terms below have a defined Ryva meaning. Teams should use them consistently in standards, product interfaces, curriculum, assessment, and public explanations.

## A

**Advanced Sales Program** — Optional professional development beyond the minimum certification, focused on additional coaching and sales mastery.

**Applied judgment** — The ability to use principles and evidence in unfamiliar or ambiguous conditions and explain the resulting decision.

## B

**Brand** — An entity offering a Product and seeking suitable business distribution or commercial relationships.

**Brand Placement** — The professional practice of identifying high-potential Products, matching them with qualified Business Buyers, and creating long-term commercial relationships through independent representation.

**Brand Placement Representative / Representative** — An independent professional who identifies, evaluates, develops, and supports relationships between Brands and qualified Businesses.

**Business** — An organization evaluating or purchasing a Product for a legitimate business purpose.

**Business Buyer / Buyer** — A person authorized to evaluate or make a purchasing decision for a Business.

**Business fit** — The contextual alignment of Product and Brand with a Business's customers, positioning, economics, channel, operations, and timing.

## C

**Candidate** — A person seeking Ryva certification. In the Opportunity lifecycle, a potential match still requiring research; the context must make the meaning clear.

**Certification** — Ryva's time-limited private professional credential validating defined Brand Placement competencies.

**Certified Representative** — A Representative whose Ryva certification is current and in good standing.

**Competency** — A defined combination of knowledge, skill, judgment, and conduct that can be demonstrated and assessed.

**Continuing education** — Approved learning undertaken to maintain or extend competence after initial certification.

**CRM** — The relationship and professional-record component of Ryva Pro; not a complete definition of Ryva Pro.

## E

**Evidence** — Information used to support or challenge a fact, score, inference, recommendation, or decision.

## G

**Good standing** — Current certification status meeting ethics, continuing-education, designation-use, and disciplinary requirements.

## I

**Independent representation** — The commercial practice of representing a Brand without manufacturing the Product, owning inventory, or operating the purchasing Business. It does not by itself determine legal employment or agency status.

**Inference** — A conclusion derived from evidence but not directly observed as fact.

## M

**Marketplace** — A network capability through which qualified participants may discover or pursue Opportunities under defined rules.

**Match thesis** — The documented professional rationale connecting a Brand and Product to a specific Business and Opportunity.

## O

**Opportunity** — A documented potential Brand–Business relationship with a credible match thesis, identifiable next step, and sufficient authority to pursue evaluation.

**Opportunity Network** — Ryva's system for connecting qualified participants to suitable commercial opportunities.

## P

**Placement** — A Brand–Business commercial relationship developed through Brand Placement. The term must not imply a guaranteed order or ongoing result.

**Product** — The good or offering evaluated for a Brand–Business relationship.

**Professional Development** — Learning and guided practice that maintains or extends professional competence, including continuing education and optional advanced programs.

## R

**Relationship stewardship** — Ongoing support, monitoring, communication, and review after an introduction or commercial commitment.

**Renewal** — The process by which a certified Representative extends certification by meeting updated standards, continuing education, and good-standing requirements.

**Ryva** — The professional-standard, certification, product, development, and network organization for Brand Placement.

**Ryva Pro** — The operating system for Brand Placement, available only to certified Representatives subject to applicable access requirements.

## S

**Score** — A versioned, evidence-based comparative result. A score is not a guarantee, entitlement, or substitute for professional judgment.

**Standard** — An approved, enduring requirement or principle within The Ryva Standard.

## T

**Three-party test** — Evaluation of whether a relationship or decision creates credible and sustainable value for the Brand, Business, and Representative.

## U

**Unknown** — Information not established by sufficient evidence. Unknown is not neutral or average evidence.

## Usage rules

- Use **Brand Placement** for the profession, not as a synonym for advertising product placement.
- Use **Business** or **Business Buyer** rather than “retailer” when the intended scope includes business purchasers beyond retail.
- Use **certification**, not “license.”
- Use **Opportunity** only when the defined minimum conditions exist.
- Use **score** as decision support, not as proof or guarantee.
- Use **AI-assisted** or a more precise description when automation materially contributes; do not imply a human performed work they did not perform.
