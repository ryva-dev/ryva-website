## Version 1.0

**Status:** Founding standard

Version 1.0 establishes:

- Ryva's identity, mission, vision, values, and four pillars;
- the definition, purpose, boundaries, lifecycle, and ethics of Brand Placement;
- Representative conduct and competence;
- the private certification model, two-year term, renewal philosophy, and assessment principles;
- curriculum and continuing-development philosophy;
- the intended role and boundaries of Ryva Pro;
- CRM, data, AI, Product intelligence, Brand intelligence, and scoring doctrine;
- the Opportunity Network, marketplace, and community philosophy;
- decision, roadmap, governance, and change-control standards;
- controlled terminology; and
- a consolidated register of unresolved founder decisions.

No prior version of The Ryva Standard is declared by this repository.
