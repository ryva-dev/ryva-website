# Completed Review — FP-011 Vague Commission Terms

**Case:** [FP-011](../cases/FP-011-vague-commission.md)  
**Outcome represented:** Proceed With Conditions  
**Framework version:** Internal pilot v0.1  
**Reviewer:** Synthetic worked example

## 1. Evidence Standard

| Input | Classification | Supports | Does not support |
|---|---|---|---|
| E1: wholesale invoices and reorders | Direct Evidence | Existing wholesale and repeat activity | Future Buyer performance |
| E2: complete Product/readiness materials | Direct Evidence / Verified current facts | Current operational readiness | Representation authority |
| E3: 94% on-time export | Direct Evidence with first-party limitation | Observed delivery performance | Reliability in every future account |
| E4: “around 12%” email | Verified Fact about a vague proposal | Material ambiguity in Representative economics | Enforceable commission right or rate |
| E5: Slate & Stem observations | Strong Proxy for preliminary Business Fit | Credible target-research hypothesis | Buyer need or intent |
| E6: no agreement/contact | Verified Fact about current state | Represent gate incomplete | Negative Product or Brand quality |

**Additional material inputs**

- “The founder intends fair compensation” is an **Assumption**.
- “The ambiguity can be resolved before external activity” is an **Assumption** tested through negotiation.
- “Slate & Stem is likely to fit” is an **Assumption** based on a Strong Proxy.
- No material **Weak Proxy**, **Estimate**, or **Model-Generated Inference** controls the review.

**Unsupported claims**

- “The commission is 12%.”
- “Large accounts will be paid fairly.”
- “The Representative owns Slate & Stem.”
- “Product strength makes written terms unnecessary.”

**Decision-controlling unknowns**

- commission rate, calculation basis, exclusions, adjustments, payment trigger and timing;
- account attribution, house accounts, territory, post-termination, and disputes;
- actual representation authority;
- Buyer need and decision process.

## 2. Human Judgment Standard

**AI may assist with**

- extracting all missing commercial terms;
- comparing draft clauses once supplied;
- organizing Product and operational records;
- preparing target research without external contact.

**Requires human review**

- commercial fairness and ambiguity;
- mandate, conflict, territory, and account attribution;
- acceptance or rejection of representation;
- final Buyer-fit and outreach.

**Cannot be delegated**

- negotiation and acceptance of commission terms;
- representation acceptance;
- claims to the Buyer;
- final outreach, negotiation, closing, or conflict decision.

**Representative-owned decision**

The Representative may continue internal negotiation and non-contact target research but cannot accept representation or contact the Buyer.

**Specialist review**

Legal or financial review may be appropriate if the eventual agreement contains material ambiguity, unusual adjustment, or dispute terms. It is not necessary merely to identify that the current email is insufficient.

## 3. Placement Cycle

**Current stage:** Represent.

**Completed entry criteria**

- Product and Brand evidence support mandate consideration;
- current readiness appears adequate;
- preliminary future target hypothesis exists.

**Missing exit criteria**

- complete written mandate;
- exact commission and payment rules;
- territory, account, conflict, authority, duration, and termination terms;
- accepted responsibilities and escalation.

**Next required action**

Convert the vague proposal into a written term sheet or agreement and resolve every decision-controlling field. Internal target research may continue without Buyer contact.

**Prohibited premature action**

Contact Slate & Stem, identify as Representative, record a protected account, or perform commission-bearing work.

## 4. Relationship Triangle

| Party | Potential value | Obligations | Risks / warning signs | Condition |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Brand | Credible expansion through a prepared Representative | Define authority, support, economics, and account rules | Vague terms can create disputes and unreliable coverage | Supported for negotiation |
| Business Buyer | Potential fit with ready Product | Evaluate actual need and economics when lawfully approached | No current relationship or need evidence | Conditional |
| Representative | Possible fair compensation and portfolio fit | Negotiate clear terms, remain within authority, avoid premature work | Compensation and account attribution are Unknown | Conditional |

**Can all three receive legitimate value?** Potentially, if the mandate and economics become explicit before external work.

## 5. Decision Filter

| Test | Result | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Legitimate Brand value | Pass | Representation discussion can improve readiness |
| Legitimate Buyer value | Conditional | Preliminary fit only |
| Representative professionalism | Pass under conditions | Refusing vague acceptance protects professionalism |
| Long-term relationships | Conditional | Clear account and support rules are necessary |
| Evidence support | Pass for negotiation; Fail for acceptance | Product evidence is strong; mandate evidence is not |
| Trust protection | Pass if external work is blocked | No authority is implied |
| Efficiency without replacing judgment | Pass | AI may extract terms; human negotiates |
| Alignment with Standard | Pass under conditions | Authority and reliability remain gates |
| Public defensibility | Pass | Ryva can defend asking for clear terms |
| Improves profession beyond short-term revenue | Pass | Professional economics and ownership rules precede sales activity |

## 6. Final decision

**Decision:** Proceed With Conditions  
**Scope:** Internal mandate negotiation and non-contact target research only.  
**Conditions:** Written exact commission, payment, adjustments, attribution, territory, conflicts, authority, responsibilities, term, termination, and dispute rules.  
**Rationale:** Product and Brand evidence justify continued discussion, while Representative value and authority remain conditional.  
**Confidence:** Supported for conditional negotiation.  
**Required next action:** Obtain and review a complete written proposal.  
**Owner:** Representative and Brand founder for terms  
**Review trigger:** Complete written mandate.

## 7. Pilot observations

- The Relationship Triangle permits a Conditional Representative side during negotiation but not during external representation.
- Ryva Pro should separate “mandate negotiation” from “active representation.”
- “Protected Account” must not be created from target research alone.
