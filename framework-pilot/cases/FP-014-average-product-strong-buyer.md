# FP-014: Average Product With an Unusually Strong Business Buyer

**Synthetic case:** All names and facts are fictional.

## Case facts

**Case ID:** FP-014  
**Product summary:** “PlainKind Desk Kits” contain a notebook, pen, cable tie, and reusable pouch. Quality is adequate but undifferentiated.  
**Brand summary:** PlainKind Office is reliable, transparent, wholesale ready, and able to customize packaging at moderate volume.  
**Potential Business Buyer:** BrightWorks People Operations, a corporate gifting Buyer seeking 1,500 practical onboarding kits with custom sleeves and delivery to one fulfillment center.  
**Buyer type:** Corporate gifting buyer  
**Decision required:** Does exceptional Buyer fit and customization make a bounded placement reasonable despite average Product differentiation?

## Missing evidence

- approved custom sample and quality check;
- final price, margin, commission, returns, and damage terms;
- evidence of employee use or satisfaction;
- whether one-time order can create broader relationship value.

## Source type and overall evidence confidence

Buyer need and Brand operational history are Supported. Custom execution is Limited until a sample is approved. Overall decision confidence is **Supported for a bounded sample step**, not for final placement.

## Known risks

- undifferentiated Product could create limited end-user value;
- custom packaging may fail or create waste;
- large one-time order could be mistaken for durable relationship value;
- acceptable quality may or may not meet the Buyer's actual standard.

## Commercial assumptions

- Functional simplicity is legitimate value in this specific use case.
- Brand reliability transfers to the custom configuration.
- The Buyer may value operational certainty more than Product distinction.

## Ethical concerns

The Representative must not inflate Product quality or characterize a one-time order as proven long-term value.

## Current Placement Cycle stage

**Present.** Buyer evaluation is active; a custom sample and final terms are required before Place.

## Decision required

Determine whether to proceed to a bounded custom-sample and commercial-validation step. Document reasonable disagreement.
