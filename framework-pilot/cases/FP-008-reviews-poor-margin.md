# FP-008: Excellent Reviews With Poor Wholesale Margin

**Synthetic case:** All names and facts are fictional.

## Case facts

**Case ID:** FP-008  
**Product summary:** “MorningMilled Oat Cups” are shelf-stable breakfast cups with strong consumer reviews.  
**Brand summary:** MorningMilled Foods has reliable operations but proposes wholesale terms close to its direct consumer price.  
**Potential Business Buyer:** Market Row, a three-store independent grocer.  
**Buyer type:** Independent grocer  
**Decision required:** Is Product enthusiasm enough to justify pursuit under the proposed economics?

| E1 | 4.9/5 from 2,100 labeled verified purchasers | Review platform | 18 months | Supported |
| E2 | Brand has fulfilled 97% of orders on time | Operational export | 12 months | Supported |
| E3 | Wholesale unit price is $3.20; Brand suggested retail is $3.99 | Current price sheet | Current | Verified Fact |
| E4 | Comparable cups at Market Row retail for $3.49–$4.29 | Store observation | Current | Supported |
| E5 | Freight is paid by the Buyer and MOQ is 12 cases of 12 | Current wholesale terms | Current | Verified Fact |
| E6 | Brand offers the Representative 15% commission on collected wholesale revenue | Draft commission sheet | Current | Direct evidence of proposed terms |

## Missing evidence

- whether the Brand can change price, pack, freight, or promotion;
- Market Row's required category margin and shelf-turn expectations;
- returns, spoilage, and markdown policy;
- actual comparable wholesale reorders.

## Source type and overall evidence confidence

Sources include verified reviews, Brand operations and terms, store observations, and draft commission terms. Confidence in current poor margin potential is **Supported**.

## Known risks

- Buyer margin may be structurally unworkable;
- freight and MOQ further weaken economics;
- commission could reward pursuit despite Buyer disadvantage.

## Commercial assumptions

- Strong reviews will justify lower Business margin.
- Market Row may accept the Product as a traffic builder.

## Ethical concerns

The Representative must not conceal margin weakness or treat Product popularity as a reason for the Buyer to accept poor economics.

## Current Placement Cycle stage

**Evaluate.** Commercial viability has not passed qualification.

## Decision required

Determine whether to stop under current terms or investigate a commercially workable structure.
