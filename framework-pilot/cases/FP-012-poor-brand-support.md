# FP-012: Strong Product With Poor Brand Support

**Synthetic case:** All names and facts are fictional.

## Case facts

**Case ID:** FP-012  
**Product summary:** “CloudRest Travel Wrap” is a convertible travel blanket with strong verified reviews and low return rates.  
**Brand summary:** CloudRest Supply has credible Product operations but routinely takes two to three weeks to answer Representative questions and has outdated sales materials.  
**Potential Business Buyer:** Lumen Stay Hotel Shop, which needs reliable seasonal replenishment.  
**Buyer type:** Hotel shop  
**Decision required:** Should Product strength outweigh Brand communication and support weakness?
