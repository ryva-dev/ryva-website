# FP-009: Reseller With Unclear Brand Ownership

**Synthetic case:** All names and facts are fictional.

## Case facts

**Case ID:** FP-009  
**Product summary:** “Mizu Rest Tea Sets” are ceramic tea service sets labeled with the Mizu Rest name.  
**Brand summary:** Coastline Trade LLC claims to be the exclusive North American distributor but is not the trademark owner shown in public records.  
**Potential Business Buyer:** Linden Quay Hotel Shop.  
**Buyer type:** Hotel shop  
**Decision required:** Does Coastline Trade have sufficient authority for the Representative to represent the Product?

## Available evidence

|---|---|---|---|---|
| E1 | Coastline provides a state business registration and wholesale price sheet | Authoritative registry and company document | Current | Supported for entity existence |
| E2 | Public trademark record lists a Japanese company as Mizu Rest owner | Authoritative public record | Current | Verified Fact |
| E3 | Coastline's founder says exclusivity was agreed “by email years ago” | Founder statement | Current | Limited |
| E4 | No distribution agreement, authorization letter, or approved trademark-use evidence is provided | Case record | Current | Supported as missing |

| E6 | Packaging contains Japanese contact details and no Coastline distributor reference | Physical sample | Current | Supported |

## Missing evidence

- valid distribution and representation authority;
- scope, territory, term, and trademark rights;
- Product authenticity and supply provenance;
- warranty, returns, claims, insurance, and fulfillment ownership;
- correction of the Buyer's ownership assumption.

## Source type and overall evidence confidence

Sources include authoritative registries, company documents, founder assertion, sample, and Buyer note. Entity existence is Supported; representation authority is **Insufficient**.

## Known risks

- unauthorized representation or trademark use;
- counterfeit or gray-market supply;
- Buyer misunderstanding;
- unclear responsibility for Product issues.

## Commercial assumptions

- Coastline's long history of sales implies authority.
- Buyer interest justifies resolving documents after the approach.

## Ethical concerns

The Representative must correct the Buyer misunderstanding and cannot imply ownership or authority without evidence.

## Current Placement Cycle stage

**Represent.** Discovery and evaluation occurred, but the representation-authorization exit gate is not met.

## Decision required

Determine whether any external pursuit may continue and what proof is required.
