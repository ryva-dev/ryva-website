# FP-001: Viral Pet Toy Without a Wholesale Program

**Synthetic case:** All names and facts are fictional.

## Case facts

**Case ID:** FP-001  
**Product summary:** “OrbitPup Spinner,” a rechargeable motion toy for dogs, has visible short-form-video attention and a soft silicone shell.  
**Brand summary:** OrbitPup is an 11-month-old direct-to-consumer Brand. Its website offers consumer orders but no wholesale page, line sheet, case packs, or Business terms.  
**Potential Business Buyer:** Paws & Pine, a two-location independent pet store known for demonstration-friendly enrichment Products.  
**Buyer type:** Independent pet store  
**Decision required:** Should the Representative continue investigating the Product and Brand for possible representation and eventual fit with Paws & Pine?
