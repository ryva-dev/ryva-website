# FP-006: Small Brand With Strong Packaging and Margins

**Synthetic case:** All names and facts are fictional.

## Case facts

**Case ID:** FP-006  
**Product summary:** “Fieldnote Linen Mist” is a home-fragrance spray in shelf-ready amber glass packaging.  
**Brand summary:** Quiet Acre Goods has low sales and operates from a small studio but provides complete wholesale materials and disciplined pricing.  
**Potential Business Buyer:** Wren House, an independent lifestyle boutique.  
**Buyer type:** Boutique  
**Decision required:** Does limited sales history justify stopping, or should the Representative continue a controlled qualification?

## Available evidence

| ID | Available information | Source type | Period | Evidence confidence supplied to reviewer |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| E1 | Brand invoices show 420 units sold, including 90 wholesale units to three stores | Brand invoices | 12 months | Supported |
| E2 | Wholesale price is $9 and suggested retail is $22; MOQ is 24 mixed units | Current line sheet | Current | Supported |
| E3 | Physical sample has barcode, ingredient disclosure, use directions, warning, and protective shipper | Sample inspection | Current | Supported |
| E4 | Shipment records show all 17 wholesale shipments dispatched within stated five-day lead time | Brand shipping export | 12 months | Supported but small sample |
| E5 | Wren House sells adjacent home fragrance at $18–$30 | Business assortment observation | Current | Supported |
| E6 | The Brand has no paid trend data and fewer than 2,000 social followers | Public platform | Current | Supported but weak relevance |

## Missing evidence

- broader return and complaint history;
- production capacity above current volume;
- executed mandate, commission, and territory;
- Wren House Buyer need and scent preferences;
- category-specific insurance review.

## Source type and overall evidence confidence

Sources include invoices, line sheet, sample, shipment export, Business observation, and public social data. Overall confidence for controlled qualification is **Supported**.

## Known risks

- small operating scale;
- limited Product and account history;
- glass shipping damage could rise at volume;
- fragrance preference may be narrow.

## Commercial assumptions

- Strong packaging and margin may compensate for limited sales evidence.
- Wren House customers may accept the scent and price.

## Ethical concerns

The Brand must not be rejected solely because it is small, and low follower count must not be treated as lack of Product potential.

## Current Placement Cycle stage

**Qualify.** Product and Brand evidence is credible but representation and target evidence remain incomplete.

## Decision required

Determine the bounded next action and the conditions for a controlled Buyer-fit test.
