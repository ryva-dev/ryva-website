# FP-004: Trend Novelty With Weak Repeat Potential

**Synthetic case:** All names and facts are fictional.

## Case facts

**Case ID:** FP-004  
**Product summary:** “MoodMelt Reveal Candles” reveal a humorous message after approximately 40 minutes of burning.  
**Brand summary:** FlickerPlot launched four months ago and sells mainly through creator affiliate links.  
**Potential Business Buyer:** ParcelJoy, a monthly humor-themed subscription box.  
**Buyer type:** Subscription box  
**Decision required:** Should the Representative pursue this one-cycle placement despite weak evidence of repeat purchase?

## Available evidence

| ID | Available information | Source type | Period | Evidence confidence supplied to reviewer |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| E1 | 3.2 million views across 24 affiliate videos | Brand affiliate dashboard | 45 days | Supported for attributed platform activity |
| E2 | 7,400 units sold, with 41% attributed to one creator and heavy launch discounting | Brand order export | 45 days | Supported |
| E3 | 2.1% of customers placed another order; only one Product exists | Brand cohort export | 4 months | Limited due to short period |
| E4 | Subscription-box Buyer requests 2,000 units for a possible holiday theme if delivered in six weeks | Buyer email | Current | Direct evidence of conditional interest |
| E5 | Brand reports capacity of 1,200 units per month without supporting production records | Founder statement | Current | Limited |

## Missing evidence

- verified production capacity and delivery plan;
- candle category safety, labeling, insurance, and testing evidence;
- final price, margin, samples, returns, and quality at 2,000-unit volume;
- whether novelty remains relevant at delivery;
- future Product line or credible reorder path.

## Source type and overall evidence confidence

Sources include affiliate and order exports, cohort data, a Buyer email, and founder assertion. Overall confidence is **Limited**.

## Known risks

- trend concentration and discount dependence;
- weak repeat behavior;
- production capacity below requested volume;
- seasonal timing and category-specific safety.

## Commercial assumptions

- The holiday theme will remain relevant.
- The Brand can expand capacity safely.
- A one-time placement may still create legitimate value despite limited reorder potential.

## Ethical concerns

The Representative must not imply production capacity, durable demand, or reorder value that is not supported.

## Current Placement Cycle stage

**Qualify.** Conditional Buyer interest exists, but Brand readiness and category evidence are incomplete.

## Decision required

Determine whether to proceed, condition a limited order path, investigate further, or stop.
