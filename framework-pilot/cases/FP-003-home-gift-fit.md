# FP-003: Modest Sales With Strong Physical-Retail Fit

**Synthetic case:** All names and facts are fictional.

## Case facts

**Case ID:** FP-003  
**Product summary:** “Harbor Fold Trivets” are compact, folding hardwood trivets with a clear tabletop demonstration and giftable packaging.  
**Brand summary:** Alder Finch Studio is a small owner-operated Brand with two years of modest direct sales and six successful local wholesale accounts.  
**Potential Business Buyer:** Hearthline Gifts, an independent gift shop serving home entertainers and seasonal gift buyers.  
**Buyer type:** Gift shop  
**Decision required:** Should the Representative proceed to seek a representation mandate and prepare Hearthline as a target after authorization?

## Missing evidence

- executed representation authority, commission, territory, and conflict terms;
- Hearthline Buyer need, assortment gap, MOQ acceptance, and decision timing;
- verified return and complaint data beyond the six accounts;
- Brand capacity if demand grows materially.

## Source type and overall evidence confidence

Sources include Brand operational records, physical samples, wholesale invoices, Business public information, and Representative visit notes. Overall confidence for bounded next action is **Supported**.

## Known risks

- Brand is small and capacity at higher volume is untested;
- Buyer fit is a supported hypothesis, not Buyer intent;
- representation economics and authority are not yet agreed.

## Commercial assumptions

- Current price structure appears to support workable Business margin.
- Demonstrability and giftable packaging may suit Hearthline.
- Reorder behavior across four accounts may transfer to a similar shop.

## Ethical concerns

The Representative must not contact Hearthline or imply representation before a mandate exists, and must not convert small-sample reorder evidence into a broad guarantee.

## Current Placement Cycle stage

**Qualify.** Product and Brand evidence supports continued diligence; representation authorization is the next gate.

## Decision required

Decide whether to proceed with representation discussions and, only after authorization, target validation with Hearthline.
